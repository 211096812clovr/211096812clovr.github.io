On some days, I wake up feeling really happy. Others, I am miserable beyond belief. I love anticipating something, so I feel like there's something I'm waiting for , something better coming up. Whenever I'm having fun, I imagine that maybe there is some sort of hope left. But normalcy always returns in the following days, and I feel depressed again. My primary daily emotion must be shame and embarrassment. I am too embarrassed to do most things anymore. I still try so hard to put myself out there despite this, but I overthink every word or breath I take. I physically cringe when I remember something mildly embarrassing I did, because I'm unfit to experience life. I am way too sensitive and anxiety-ridden to ever enjoy anything besides being alone in my room. I will always be too overwhelmed by any stimuli I encounter outdoors, in pain from moving my body, and overthinking everything I'm doing. My body feels stiff and rigid when I'm in public, as if I were learning to act human for the first time.
